The goal of the game is to take all of your opponent’s card then slap a Jack to win the game!
GOOD SLAPS: Jack on top / Top two cards match (Double) / Of the top 3 cards, if the top and bottom card match (Sandwich)
Each player takes a turn and deals one (1) card to the center.
You can slap any card, but BEWARE, if it’s not a JACK, DOUBLE, or SANDWICH, you add your top card to the bottom of your opponents pile!
If one player is out of cards, they can stay in the game, but can only slap a Jack (No doubles or sandwiches). Or else they lose!
